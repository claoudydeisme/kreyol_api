# Ethics, Data, and Privacy

This project is built as a **public good**.

---

## Data Handling

- Input and output pairs are logged in anonymized form
- No personal identifiers are stored
- Hashing is used for traceability without reversibility

---

## Datasets

- Human-corrected translations may be published
- Datasets are released periodically
- Licensing favors open public use (e.g., CC-BY)

---

## Safety Commitments

- No hallucinations
- No added or altered intent
- No medical, legal, or financial advice
- No bias amplification

---

## Human Oversight

- Low-confidence translations are flagged
- Human corrections improve future quality
- Governance decisions are reviewable

---

## Guiding Principle

Language access is a matter of **dignity, safety, and equity**.
